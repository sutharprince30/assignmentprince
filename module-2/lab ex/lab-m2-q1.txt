extensively used, such as in embedded systems, operating systems, or game
development.

ans=

1.Embedded Systems – C is used in microcontrollers, IoT devices, and automotive systems for performance and efficiency.

3.Game Development – C is used in game engines like Unity (for low-level systems) and Unreal Engine for performance optimization.
